I own a MSI GT72VR-6RE notebook with Nvidia GTX1070 GPU. Unfortunately, the 2016 models have defective GPU PCBs. The problem rises almost at any demanding software and/or game and it is basically caused by GPU crashing for some reason. Decreasing the power consumption by under-clocking GPU and GDDR5s could be helpful to decrease the sevearity of the problem but surely it is not the solution. MSI has offered some VBIOS files to fix the problem which none of them were any good. Here in this post, you will find out about my observations of the problem and the approaches that I have taken to alleviate the issue to some level.
