phd student · HPC · inference acceleration · FPGAs · embedded

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Salerno, supervised by Prof. Biagio Cosenza. My work is on high-performance computing — optimizing ML and scientific workloads on Intel hardware and software stacks, and more broadly across GPUs, SIMD, and FPGAs.

Before the PhD I did an MSc in Electrical Engineering (IC design) at the University of Tabriz, where I worked on 3D deep learning on heterogeneous systems — GPU/FPGA acceleration of DGCNN on point clouds. I also spent a few years building embedded and IoT systems in industry.
